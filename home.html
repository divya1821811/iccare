About The Conference

Institution

PBR Visvodaya Institute of Technology and Science, an Autonomous Institution, situated in an eco-friendly environment with state-of-the-art infrastructure.

Dr. Doda Ramachandra Reddy established Visvodaya Society in 1952 at Kavali. PBR Visvodaya Institute of Technology & Science was established under the aegis of Visvodaya Society in 1988 with an initial intake of 180 students in the Undergraduate courses.

the total sanctioned intake is 1710, including 1560 in UG programmes and 150 in PG programmes. The college has achieved prestigious accreditation from NBA and NAAC. The institution also received autonomous status during the academic year 2021-22

Departments

The blooming field of study, Department of CSE Allied Branches of CSE have collaborated with Computer Science and Engineering to organize the international conference to provide a platform for the growing interdisciplinary engineering.

The CSE department is offering:

  • UG program with an intake of 480 students
  • PG program with 18 intake

Allied branches include:

  • CSE-AI with an intake of 360 seats
  • CSE-IoT branch with an intake of 120 seats
  • AI&ML branch with an intake of 360 seats

The departments are equipped with modern research tools and industry-supported laboratories (e.g., Virtusa CoE).


The Conference

The ICCARE-2025 aims to encourage interdisciplinary research and collaboration, exploring the intersection of computing and engineering with other fields.

It provides a platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to share their knowledge and experiences in computing and advanced research in engineering trends and latest innovations covering all the recent developments of Computer science and Engineering.

The conference will have keynote address from illustrious speakers and presentations of peer-reviewed high quality technical papers.

Jointly organised by Department of CSE & Allied
